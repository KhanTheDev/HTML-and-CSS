Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driver's license, credit card, and proof of insurance. International renters may need additional documentation.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your rental start time for a full refund. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Do you offer airport pickup and drop-off?

Yes, we provide convenient airport pickup and drop-off services at major airports. Please specify this when making your reservation.

What happens if I return the car late?

We offer a 1-hour grace period. After that, you'll be charged for an additional day. Please contact us if you need to extend your rental.
